SolarUp FAQ
How does SolarUp estimate the potential earnings accurately?
SolarUp uses custom api that consider various factors, including your location, weather data, panel efficiency, and local energy rates, to provide precise earnings estimates.
What benefits does SolarUp offer for the environment?
By converting parking lots and other spaces into solar energy sources, SolarUp helps reduce carbon emissions and contributes to a cleaner, more sustainable environment.
How can businesses and property owners maximize their profits with SolarUp?
SolarUp provides insights into the potential earnings from solar projects. Businesses can use this information to make informed decisions about investing in renewable energy and maximizing their profits.
Are solar canopies viable in less sunny parts of the US?
Yes! According to research done by Dr. Pearce in his study of "Technical photovoltaic potential of scaled parking lot canopies" prove the viability of this technology.
